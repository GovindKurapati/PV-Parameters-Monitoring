Existing Problem

System uses third-party apps to show performance data.
Cannot rely on the consistency of the data received.
Difficulty in comprehending the data obtained.

Solution

Visualize the data

This system enables record of electrical units generated on day to day basis, with a maximum data for that can be visualised for 5 days.
The details on electricity generation on the particular day will be available till the time of recording the data.
The data on electricity generation can be recorded both in terms of electricity units and cost equivalent.

Maintenance

The page pops up relevant message with apt color code, to reveal the exact condition of the system
Green colored message shows healthy condition of the system.
Red colored message conveys requirement of immediate attention to restore normal functioning of the system.

Sit back and Relax

The system designed through this project helps in regular monitoring of the condition of all the panels from a comfortable single point.
This will help in reduction of manpower requirement, manual efforts and thus cost involved in manpower management.
